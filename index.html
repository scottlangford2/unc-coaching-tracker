How to Spot It

ADS-B Exchange — RDU ADS-B — IGX (UNC Campus) ADS-B — TTA (Raleigh Exec) FlightAware RDU Arrivals
  • UNC planes: Barons and TBMs based at RDU. But they may use charters to hide.
  • Booster planes: Search FAA by name of known boosters or their companies.
  • Charter/fractional: NetJets, Wheels Up, Flexjet — watch the route even if you can't ID the owner.
  • Shell LLCs: Some owners register planes under LLCs. Cross-reference LLC addresses with Chapel Hill/NC.
  • ADS-B Exchange shows ALL planes. FlightAware/FR24 let owners hide — use ADS-B Exchange first.
  • Look for round trips under 12 hours. Late night/early morning flights. Gulfstreams, Citations, King Airs.
